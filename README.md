# Tour Management App

A comprehensive web application designed to manage tours, bookings, customer details, and payments efficiently. This app is aimed at tour companies and agencies looking to streamline their operations and provide an enhanced experience for customers.

## Features

- **User Registration & Authentication**: Secure login, registration, and profile management for customers.
- **Tour Listings**: Detailed information on tours, including destinations, itineraries, prices, and available slots.
- **Booking Management**: Booking creation, modification, and cancellation features.
- **Payment Integration**: Secure payment gateway for booking and reservations.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Manage users, tours, bookings, and payments in a single dashboard.
- **Search & Filters**: Easily search and filter tours by destination, price, and other parameters.
- **Notifications**: Email and in-app notifications for booking confirmations, cancellations, and reminders.
